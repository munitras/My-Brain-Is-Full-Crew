# Startup Instructions

Welcome back to your vault. Automatically running the Librarian's health check and the Foreman's task sweep to provide your status dashboard.

- **Foreman Sweep**: Execute `bash scripts/foreman-sweep.sh . > Meta/Foreman-Tasks.md` to ensure the task list is fresh.
- **Check Vault Health**: Perform a quick health check of the vault.
- **Display Dashboard**: Summarize the current state of the inbox, pending messages, and recent health reports, and advise the user to "Please check the Dashboard HUD for your tasks today."
